MIT (Applied) Categories Seminar

The MIT categories seminar is an informal research seminar in category theory and categorical modelling. We meet each Thursday, 4.30pm to 5.30pm in MIT 2-255. Sometimes notes from the seminar are published here: https://categoricalmodeling.wordpress.com/.
